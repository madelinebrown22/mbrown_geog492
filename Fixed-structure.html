The Next 3 Winter Olympic Cities

Milan

In 2026, Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo are going to host the 2026 Winter Olympics. Cortina d'Ampezzo previously hosted the Olympics in 1956 and 1944. Milan will primarily be used for indoor events and Cortina d'Ampezzo will be used for mountain sports.

Briançon (French Alps)

In 2030, the Winter Olympics will be held in the French Alps. Many cities will be utilized to host the events, but so far Briançon is most notable. France has hosted the Olympics in 1900, 1924, 1968, 1992, and 2024.

Salt Lake City

Salt Lake City, Utah will host the Winter Olympics in 2034. Salt Lake City previously hosted the Winter Olympics in 2002. The US has hosted 8 Olympic games, more than any other nation.
